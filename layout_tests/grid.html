Verbs follow certain rules.

Here I have some tips for you to learning Finnish
Make Finnish even funnier to... easily remember !
  1. Think of Finnish as a puzzle, rather than a language.
  2. Watch kids TV and read kids books
  3. Read textbooks but write in a notebook
  4. Write letters, postcards, articles, or whatever in Finnish and ask your teacher to check them for you.
  5. Writing a diary in Finnish
  6. Speak Finnish as often as possible
  7. Read text aloud !

IMPORTANT POINTS: In my Finnish study journey, there is one theory that I have always kept in my mind. I pay special attention to the spelling and pronunciation of words. Why? What are the benefits of this? If you know the correct spelling and basic grammar, it helps you to read and write texts faster and more accurately.
