From Vietnam to Finland

Situated in Southeast Asia, Vietnam boasts a tropical climate characterized by hot and humid summers, and mild, wet winters. In contrast, Finland, nestled in Northern Europe, experiences a subarctic climate with long, cold winters and short, mild summers. Despite their geographical and meteorological disparities, both nations possess rich cultural heritages and offer unique experiences for travelers seeking diverse landscapes and climates. From the bustling streets of Hanoi to the tranquil forests of Finland, each country promises an unforgettable journey.
